Responsible material communication

Better claims begin with exact product facts

“Eco-friendly” is not a complete specification. Jaxbin encourages buyers to identify material composition, sourcing evidence, food-contact use and realistic end-of-life routes for each SKU.

Our approach

Material first, claim second

1
Wood and bamboo

Review species, source, surface treatment, adhesives, coatings and packaging.

2
Bagasse fiber

Confirm fiber formulation, additives, barrier requirements and applicable test reports.

3
Paper products

Identify board type, printing, lining or coating and local recovery options.

4
CPLA

Use industrial-compostability language only when supported and when appropriate facilities exist.

Buyer checklist

Questions to ask before publishing a claim

What exactly is it made from?

Include coatings, inks, glue, sleeves and lids—not only the main body.

Which report supports the claim?

Check product name, model, material, validity, test method and issuing body.

Where can it actually go?

Disposal systems differ by country, city and commercial waste contractor.
